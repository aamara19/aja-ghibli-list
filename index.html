all my recommendations

Kiki's Delivery Service

Kiki's Delivery Service

1989

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

my thoughts

a young witch finds her way in a new city — all about independence, self-doubt, and finding your creative spark again.

why i recommend it

if you've ever felt lost or unsure of yourself, kiki's journey will feel so personal and encouraging.

Porco Rosso

Porco Rosso

1992

⭐⭐⭐⭐

my thoughts

a world-weary pilot who's been turned into a pig flies around the adriatic. melancholic and surprisingly funny.

why i recommend it

one of miyazaki's most personal films. the vibe is completely unique — like jazz and nostalgia in movie form.

Pom Poko

Pom Poko

1994

⭐⭐⭐⭐

my thoughts

a wild, funny, and surprisingly heartbreaking story about raccoon dogs fighting to save their forest.

why i recommend it

it's unlike anything else ghibli made — hilarious and then suddenly devastating. unforgettable.

Whisper of the Heart

Whisper of the Heart

1995

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

my thoughts

a girl discovers her love of writing while chasing a mysterious boy she keeps running into at the library.

why i recommend it

the most grounded and human ghibli film. perfect for anyone who loves books, art, or has ever had a dream.

Spirited Away

Spirited Away

2001

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

my thoughts

a girl gets trapped in a spirit world and must work to save her parents. visually stunning and deeply imaginative.

why i recommend it

the most famous ghibli film for a reason — it's a masterpiece. watch it first if you're new to ghibli.

Howl's Moving Castle

Howl's Moving Castle

2004

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

my thoughts

a girl cursed to look old finds refuge in a wizard's magical moving castle. romantic, dreamy, and visually gorgeous.

why i recommend it

sophie's inner strength is so quietly powerful. the castle, calcifer, the war — everything is magical.

Ponyo

Ponyo

2008

⭐⭐⭐⭐

my thoughts

a little fish-girl wants to become human to stay with a boy she met on the shore. joyful and completely charming.

why i recommend it

the most childlike and pure ghibli film. perfect for when you need pure, uncomplicated joy in your life.

The Secret World of Arrietty

The Secret World of Arrietty

2010

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

my thoughts

tiny people living beneath a family's floorboards — the world-building is incredible and every tiny detail is lovingly crafted.

why i recommend it

it makes the mundane world feel magical and vast. one of the most beautifully designed ghibli films.

From Up on Poppy Hill

From Up on Poppy Hill

2011

⭐⭐⭐⭐

my thoughts

a girl and boy work together to save their school's clubhouse while uncovering a complicated family secret.

why i recommend it

understated and sweet — a quieter ghibli film about history, community, and gentle first love.

The Wind Rises

The Wind Rises

2013

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

my thoughts

a fictionalized life of the engineer who designed the zero fighter plane. beautiful, melancholic, and deeply personal.

why i recommend it

miyazaki's farewell to animation (at the time). if you want something more adult and reflective, this is it.

The Boy and the Heron

The Boy and the Heron

2023

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

my thoughts

a boy is drawn into a mysterious tower and an otherworldly realm after losing his mother. surreal and deeply layered.

why i recommend it

miyazaki's grand return after a decade — dense and personal. best appreciated after seeing other ghibli films first.
